03

Linescore — reassign stat-emphasis from gold to team accent

P1

Gold currently plays ~12 semantic roles — numerals, numbers, links, rules, borders. Shown this way, the winning team (and the team you're reading about) gets lost. After: gold stays for editorial chrome; the team accent earns the score.

11

Section header rule — gold gradient vs team-accent gradient

P1

Section headers carry the site's strongest decorative idea — a paper→gold→paper gradient rule. Swapping gold for team accent on each page makes this rule identify your team every time you scroll past it.
